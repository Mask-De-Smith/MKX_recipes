Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless), cut into bite sized cubes
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp cumin paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Now moving on to the cooking part of this recipe,

follow the below given steps by the dot and you should be

ready with this amazing authentic dish in max to max 2 hrs.

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Marinating and preparing the chicken

    • First collect all the chicken cubes in a decently sized bowl
    • Mix the cubes with yoghurt, chili powder, turmeric and salt
    • and let it sit for 30mins - 1 hrs, 2-4 hrs or 8-12 hrs (more time equals more flavour in the chicken).

  2. Cooking the marinatd chicken

    • Bring 2tbsp of butter to medium heat in a pan
    • Sear the chicken in the butter until lightly browned
    • Then remove and set aside the chicken

  3. Preparing the sauce

    • In the same pan, add 1tbsp of butter and then saute the ginger-garlic paste we kept aside
    • Add our tomato puree, cumin powder, and garam masala
    • Cook for 5-7 mins until the oil seperates from the sauce

  4. Combine the chicken and the sauce

    • Return the chicken we kept aside into the pan with the sauce
    • Add in our heavy cream and mix thouroughly
    • Let it simmer for 10 mins
    • Adjust salt as needed

  5. Garnish and serve

    • Take out all of the dish in a seperate bowl entirely
    • Sprinkle with fresh coriander on top
    • Server with hot naan, roti or steamed rice
